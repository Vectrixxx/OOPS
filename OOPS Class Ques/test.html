About Manipal University Jaipur

Established in 2011, Manipal University Jaipur (MUJ) is a self-financed State Private University with a vision to become a global university through excellence in teaching, research and innovation.

Located in the vibrant city of Jaipur, the university offers a vibrant learning environment with world-class infrastructure, distinguished faculty, and a focus on holistic student development.
